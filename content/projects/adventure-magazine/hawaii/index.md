## A Family Trip

This has been the exclusive ADVENTURE MAGAZINE that features my family. I hope there are more, but as it stands, this is the outlier.

Follow me, my mom, and dad as we explore the Big Island of Hawaii. Bouncing around, we visit the black sands beach, volcanic lava flows, and see countless lizards and frogs.
